肾危7征兆


肾脏正常功能最需要的物质是水。

肿胀
万一你的肾功能不正常，就以肿胀为标志。
这个迹象可以表现在脚踝，腿，手，脸和脚。
这是肾脏无法清除生物体内毒素和液体的结果，他们在已经提到的区域中进行储备。

疲劳
当我们健康的时候，我们所有的器官都运转良好，我们有能量，我们感觉很好。
一旦我们情绪低迷，我们的身体就会发生一些事情。
当肾脏不能正常工作时，可能会导致贫血、疲劳甚至脑损伤。
所以要认真对待肾脏。

皮疹
如果肾脏受损，你可能会感到：
尿的变化
尿色淡，尿频多
泡沫尿
暗尿
排尿过程中的压力
尿痛
夜间尿急

kidney danger 7 signs
The most necessary substance that kidneys need for proper function is water H2O.

Swelling
In case you have improper function of the kidney, swelling as a sign. 
This sign can be manifested on the ankle, leg, hand, face and feet. 
This is the result of kidneys inability to eliminate the toxins and liquids from the organism and they are making reserves in the already mentioned areas.

Fatigue
When we are healthy and all of our organs are working well, we have energy, we are feeling good. 
Once we are feeling down, something is happening in our organism. 
When the kidneys are not working right they may cause anemia, fatigue even a brain damage. 
So take the kidneys serious.

Skin Rashes
Except these three symptoms, if the kidney is damaged you may feel:
Changes of the urine
Pale urine color, frequent urge of urine and plenty of it
Foamy urine
Dark urine
Pressure during urination process
Pain while urinating
Intensive urge for urinating due to the night
