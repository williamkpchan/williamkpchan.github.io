PUP TO NO GOOD

These naughty dogs show why they can be man’s best fiend by destroying their homes

Owning a pet dog is never predictable and these mischievous mutts gave their stunned owners more than they bargained for

THEY’VE been pup to no good – and these mischievous mutts have guilt written all over their furry faces.

Yesterday we showed how bulldog Rocco tore into a bean bag, turning it into a has-been bag and filling the room with its stuffing.

Owner Neil Kemp then filmed his pet looking ashamed – and Rocco joined an online rogues’ gallery of four-legged fiends caught in the act.

The naughty bulldog is one of many who has been shamed by their owners after causing havoc.

Pet lovers frequently upload snaps of their four-legged friends looking guilty while surrounded by raggedy furniture, bedraggled wallpaper and shredded magazines.

Here AMY READING and NICK PRITCHARD pick some of the best.
