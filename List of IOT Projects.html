Internet of Things or IoT is an environment or network where everything and everyone are connected. Internet of Things is growing rapidly with more devices getting connected every day.

All the major engineering and IT companies are investing more money in the area of IoT and continue to invest as they understand the potential of the technology.

There are many areas of applications of the Internet of Things like consumer, health, industrial, transportation, security, entertainment and many other. More information about IoT and its applications can be found here .

The list of possible applications of IoT is endless. The following is a list of few of the many applications that are possible with IoT.
